Providing Canvas Feedback

Your feedback is a vital link in the chain that helps us to continually evolve Canvas into the robust application we have envisioned. Our goal is to know that you can confidently turn to Canvas for high quality customized screen design and development. Your user experience is important to us!

Important: This feedback program is confidential, so not only do we ask that you not share it without our permission, but we assure you that your responses will be kept in-house as well.

It's easy to provide feedback about Canvas.

In the Canvas toolbar, we have provided you with the (Submit feedback) button to seamlessly share your thoughts without interrupting your workflow.

In the Backstage view, we have provided you with a Feedback page, where you have two opportunities to click a button and share your feedback with us:

  • Submit feedback — Click (Submit feedback) to provide general feedback such as comments and impressions, enhancement ideas, or bug reports. (This is the same as using the Submit feedback button on the Canvas toolbar.) On the form provide a Summary and Description of your feedback, and then include your contact information. Adding information to the Submit Feedback form is optional, but if you do, it will be saved for future submissions. Contact information is not required for submission. Click Send to send the form or Cancel to close the form without sending.
  • Gather logs — Click (Gather logs) to zip up log and settings files and submit them to CygNet Support in a separate email.

Feedback Prompts

As an experienced CygNet user, you are in a unique position to offer informed feedback about this product, and to influence its future evolution to support your needs. All feedback will be considered, digested, and put to good use as we continue to test, refine, and develop the application.

The following table lists some areas of interest and some sample questions you might ask yourself to help generate responses about how you use, or would like to use, the Canvas application more effectively.

Comments Questions

Appearance
  • What was your first impression of the product's appearance? Any favorite aspects?
  • What details do you like/dislike about the current Canvas appearance?
  • Did you initially find anything confusing about the appearance or organization of elements?
  • Are there certain appearance aspects that helped you more quickly understand how to use it?

Usability
  • What do you think about the ease of using Canvas to create screens?
  • What was your experience configuring the workspace? Any problems or requests?
  • Are there functions that are more difficult/confusing to use than others?
  • Are there functions that are especially wonderful that you'd like others to mimic?

Features
  • What do you like/dislike about using the current Canvas features?
  • What are the hardest/easiest features to understand?
  • Do you have favorite controls or screen features that you'd like to see added/expanded/removed?
  • Which features do you use the most/least frequently?

If only…
  • If only... you could change one thing, what would it be?
  • If only... you could add one thing, what would it be?
  • If only... you could use one additional programming language, what would it be?
  • If only... thoughts of your own?
