Supported Field Devices

CygNet interfaces with many common field devices (RTUs and PLCs). The following table lists many, but not all, of the field devices CygNet supports. Although this list is extensive, CygNet also supports other less-common devices that are not listed here. Additionally, some CygNet EIEs interface with non-device-specific protocols, like DNP3 and Modbus, or with non-device-specific software, like Realflo.

Field device product names change over time as manufacturers buy other manufacturers, products are re-branded, and so forth. In some cases this means that a device is no longer known by the previous name, and will be found under its more recent name. Contact your device manufacturer for clarification of device product names.
