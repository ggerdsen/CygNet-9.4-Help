Using CygNet Measurement

CygNet Measurement involves a variety of processes, including configuring your system and devices properly, setting up administrative options and templates, retrieving and editing data, importing and exporting data, sending data to devices or SCADA, and running reports. The Flow Measurement Service (FMS) is the CygNet service that functions as CygNet Measurement's interface between the SCADA system and the database. See FMS Service for more information.

The following topics describe how to access some common CygNet Measurement functionality. CygNet Measurement Workflow provides a comprehensive overview of many common FMS tasks.
