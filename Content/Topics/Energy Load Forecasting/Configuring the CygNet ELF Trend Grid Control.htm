Configuring the CygNet ELF Trend Grid Control

The following information enables you to configure the CygNet ELF Trend Grid control to select, format, and display forecast series data.

To Add and Configure a ELF Trend Grid

  1. Create a new CygNet Studio screen.
  2. Click the ActiveX tool on the Standard Tools palette.
  3. Click and drag the outline of the trend box on the screen. When the mouse button is released, an Insert OLE Control dialog box appears.
  4. In the dialog box, double-click on CygNet ELF Trend Grid Control.
  5. Right-click the control to open its property sheet.
  6. On the ActiveX page, click … for (OCXProperties).
  7. The CygNet ELF Trend Grid control contains two property pages. See Configuring Device and Display Options and Configuring Trend Series to configure your forecast trends.
