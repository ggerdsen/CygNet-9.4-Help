Record Owner and Record Owner Group UDCs

Historical and forecast input data items will be stored in points with UDCs configured in the ELF driver. For any given input data item, two distinct UDCs will be defined to store the historical data and forecast data separately. Data items can be Ordinal (Analog) or Categorical (Enumeration).
