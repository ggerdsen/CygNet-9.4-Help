ACTIVATION_STATUS

Activation Status

An enumerated value of the host activation status. This Info Item can be used to monitor activation status. The values for SVMACTSTAT include:

  • 0 (UNKNOWN)
  • 1 (TRIAL)
  • 2 (ENABLED)
  • 3 (DISABLED)
  • 4 (EXPIRED)
  • 5 (ERROR)
  • 6 (CODE CALC ERROR)

FAILOVER_STATUS

Failover status

Indicates the current failover status of the service. The values include:

  • Not redundantâ€” For a non-redundant service.
  • Normal â€” Not currently doing a failover. Applies to all services.
  • Waiting â€” Waiting for the next step in the failover sequence. Applies to all services.
  • Freezing â€” In the process of stopping all processing. Applies to all services except RSM.
  • Frozen â€” All processing has stopped. Applies to all services except RSM.
  • Replicating â€” The service is catching up on replication. Applies to all services except RSM.
  • Processing â€” The master RSM service is doing work. Applies to the master RSM only.
  • Pre-failover â€” The RSM is waiting for all services to synchronize. Applies to RSMs only.
  • Starting â€” The service is starting a failover. Applies to RSMs only.
  • Failover â€” Switching services from standby to active. Applies to RSMs only.
  • Post-failover â€” Starting standby services and changing replication source of indirect services. Applies to RSMs only.
  • Aborting â€” Aborting failover. Applies to RSMs only.
  • Error â€” The service is in error. Applies to all services except RSM.
  • Monitoring â€” Not currently doing a failover, but monitoring the state of the RSM on the Primary Active Server. Applies to RSMs only.
  • Unknown â€” The failover status of the service is unknown.
