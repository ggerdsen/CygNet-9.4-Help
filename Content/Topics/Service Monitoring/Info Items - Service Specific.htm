Service Specific Info Items

Some service specific info items are exclusive to individual services and others are shared between two or more services. They are found on the Service Specific page of the CygNet Service Information dialog box.

Individual Service Info Items

The following info items are exclusive to individual services and are listed in the tables below.

  • ACS
  • APPS/BSS
  • ARS
  • AUD
  • CAS
  • DDS
  • ELS/ELSALM
  • FAC
  • FMS
  • GNS
  • GRP
  • MSS
  • PNT
  • RSM
  • UIS
  • VHS

Shared Service Info Items

See Shared Service Specific Info Items for a list of these info items that are shared between two or more services.
