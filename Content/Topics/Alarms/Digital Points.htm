The alarms Chattering and Digital Warning are shown for backwards compatibility. These alarms are not based on a calculation type and are not calculated by the CVS. They merely represent bits 02 and 05, respectively. The alarms can only be enabled and set through an external application or script; however, you must set the reporting and notification options using the PNT Editor.
