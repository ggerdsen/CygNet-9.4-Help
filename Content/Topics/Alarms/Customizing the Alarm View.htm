Customizing the Alarm View

The alarm view has several customization options. You can sort and filter the display, select what columns of information to view, and show the alarm color in just the alarm bell or the entire line.

You can select all the alarms in the CAS, not just those in the alarm view, by right-clicking in the alarm view and selecting Select All. To turn off Select All, click on one of the alarms to release the entire selection.

Alarm types and alarm options are based on the point type.
