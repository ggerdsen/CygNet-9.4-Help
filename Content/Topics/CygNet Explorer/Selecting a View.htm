Selecting a View

The contents of the service pane (right pane) depend on the nature of the service selected. Some of the service panes in CygNet Explorer have multiple views (tabbed pages). These are the ACS, DDS, GNS, MSS, and the UIS. Each service pane view is described in the table below.

Service Pane Contents and Properties Views

For each service, double-click on a record in its service pane, or right-click to access the context menu and then select Properties, to see its service record Properties (or other) dialog box.
