Filter Attributes - Facility Attributes

The following filter attributes represents attributes from the point’s FAC record. They are available for use throughout CygNet Software.

  • Facility: General Attributes
  • Facility: Text Attributes
  • Facility: Table-Driven Attributes
  • Facility: Yes/No Attributes
