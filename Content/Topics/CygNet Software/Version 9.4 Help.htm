Version v9.4 Help

Welcome to CygNet — The CygNet® IoT/SCADA platform collects, manages, and distributes critical real-time and operational data across your oil and gas enterprise. Using the CygNet platform, operators can process diverse data and information—from downhole sensors to surface facilities and pipelines. Users across every business function can prioritize and analyze real-time information to support strategic decision-making and optimized operations.

CygNet IoT/SCADA also serves as a foundational platform for the Weatherford Production 4.0 performance solution comprising a suite of coordinated products delivering end-to-end control, data management, and advanced data analytics across your enterprise to activate field-wide intelligence and maximize production.
